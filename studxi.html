The Solution

StudXi Makes It Simple

StudXi is designed to help students stay organized, focused, and stress-free. Everything you need — attendance tracking, task management, and timetable planning — in one place.

Features

Everything You Need to Excel

📊
Attendance Tracking

Track subject-wise attendance easily and always know your percentage. Stay aware of how many classes you can safely miss — the app calculates it for you.

✅
Task Management

Add daily study tasks and assignments. Set priorities (High, Medium, Low) and stay productive with a clean, simple to-do list that keeps you on track.

📅
Weekly Timetable

Organize your weekly class timetable. Quickly check what class comes next and never miss a lecture. Syncs directly with your attendance tracker.

📝
Exam & Study Planning

Keep track of upcoming exams. Plan your study schedule in advance and reduce last-minute stress with a clear overview of what's coming up.

⏱️
Built-in Study Timer

Stay focused with a simple study timer. Choose from 25, 45, 60, or 90 minute sessions. Build consistent study habits and track your total study time.

🏠
Productivity Hub

Your central dashboard shows your productivity score, overall attendance, priority tasks, and quick actions — everything at a glance when you open the app.
