Privacy Policy
for GrowthQuest

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

Thank you for using GrowthQuest. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our habit tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Section 1

Information We Collect

GrowthQuest is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data you enter in the app is stored locally on your device only.

Data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Habit names, icons, and colors you create
  • Daily habit completion records
  • Streak counts and XP points
  • Level and badge progress
  • App settings and preferences (including dark mode)
  • Reminder notification times
Section 2

Information We Do NOT Collect

GrowthQuest does not collect any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or unique hardware IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Any data from your contacts, camera, or microphone
Section 3

Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

GrowthQuest displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect certain information to serve relevant ads. This may include:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable device identifier)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system version
  • General location (country/region level only)
Google AdMob
This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings under Google Settings → Ads.

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Section 4

Notifications

GrowthQuest may send you local push notifications to remind you to complete your habits. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Section 5

Data Storage and Security

All your habit data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We do not have access to your data, and your data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party (except as described in Section 3 regarding AdMob).

If you uninstall GrowthQuest, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

Section 6

Children's Privacy

GrowthQuest is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, this app is safe for general audiences.

Section 7

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of GrowthQuest after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
