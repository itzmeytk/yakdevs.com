StudXi ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do Not Collect

StudXi is designed to respect user privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as:

Learn more: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Section 5

StudXi is suitable for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
